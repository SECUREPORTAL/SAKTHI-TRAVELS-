About Sakthi Travels

Mumbai's dependable name in chauffeur driven travel

Sakthi Travels was founded to bring a genuinely premium standard to chauffeur driven car rental in Mumbai — where every pickup is on time, every vehicle is immaculate, and every driver is someone you'd trust with a client, a colleague, or your own family.

Today, we serve individuals, families and corporate accounts across the city, running everything from single airport transfers to full monthly employee transportation programs, with the same attention to detail on every single trip.

Our office is based in Sion, Mumbai, and our chauffeurs and fleet are available across the city and for outstation journeys, 24 hours a day, every day of the year.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I book a chauffeur driven car with Sakthi Travels?

Simply fill out the booking enquiry form on this page, call us at +91 85912 44548, or message us on WhatsApp. You'll receive an enquiry ID instantly and our team will confirm your trip shortly after.

Do you provide airport pickup with flight tracking?

Yes. For all airport pickups we track your flight so your chauffeur adjusts pickup timing automatically, along with complimentary waiting time on arrival.

Can businesses set up a corporate billing account?

Yes, we offer dedicated corporate accounts with monthly consolidated billing. Reach out to our Accounts & Billing team at +91 90823 67845 to get started.

Do you offer outstation one-way and round-trip rentals?

Yes, both one-way drops and round trips are available for outstation journeys, with experienced highway drivers and well-maintained vehicles.

Is Sakthi Travels available 24×7?

Yes, our chauffeurs and customer support are available every day, around the clock, including holidays.

What payment options are accepted?

We accept cash, UPI, card payments and, for corporate clients, monthly invoiced billing.

How We Use Your Information
